Ingredients

  • hot dog buns
  • All Beef Franks
  • Other toppings of your choice

Recipe Steps

  1. Boil water
  2. Once water is boiling, put hot dogs into water for roughly 10 minutes.
  3. 1 minute before hot dogs are done, place the hot dog buns into the oven and toast them on 350 degrees for 3 minutes.
  4. Remove buns from oven and place one hot dog into a lightly toasted bun.
  5. Add toppings of choice.
