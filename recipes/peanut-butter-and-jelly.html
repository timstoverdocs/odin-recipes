Ingredients

  • Bread of your choice, I prefer a whole grain bread.
  • Peanut butter of your choice, I prefer Skippy Natural.
  • Jam, jelly, preserves of your choice, I prefer Smuckers Raspberry Preserves.

Recipe Steps

  1. On one slice of bread, slather peanut butter.
  2. On the other slice of bread, carefully smooth on some jam.
  3. Place bread together with the peanut butter and jam combining.
  4. The ratio of peanut butter to jam should be about 70/30.
  5. Optionally, enjoy with a cold glass of a milk alternative, I prefer almond.
