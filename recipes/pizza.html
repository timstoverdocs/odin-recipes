Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon active dry yeast
  • 2/3 Cup warm water
  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon slat
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 pound shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 cupd grated Romano cheese
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
  • 1 table spoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes
  • An appropriate amount of pepperoni

Recipe Steps

  1. Dissolve yeast in warm water with salt and oil.
  2. Mix in flour to form a dough, then let it rise for a specific time period.
  3. Roll out the dough into a crust.
  4. Spread tomato sauce, then add mozzarella, Romano, basil, oregano, red pepper flakes, and pepperoni.
  5. Bake until cheese is melted and crust is golden.
